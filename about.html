Who is Jame?

I know you wouldn't have expected this, but my real name is actually James. I grew up for the most part in Kentucky, but I've lived all around the eastern coast of the US (both parents were military). I've picked up and dropped lots of hobbies in my lifetime but the one that has stuck with me the longest has been playing video games. One of my favorite memories from my childhood is watching my dad play Halo 2 when he had a day off from work - I specifically remember the part during the Metropolis mission after you cross the bridge and exit the tunnel. I was mesmerized by the level design at the time.

My experience with playing games and watching other people play games has shaped a lot of who I am today. From making life long friendships to finding a deep appreciation for game design and the curation of interactable experinces, the social, emotional, and physical aspects of video games and their creative process have fashioned a set of entertainment-tinted lenses in which I view the world and what I can deliver to it.

"Wow this guy likes video games."

No, I do not like video games; I love video games.

So much so that I want to make video games for other people to love, to build friendships around, to learn from, to hyper fixate on, to use as a creative outlet or inspiration for their own game development endeavors.
